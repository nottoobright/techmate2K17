Laser Tag

It is a group event which will consist of 6 players i.e 3 in each team. It will consist laser guns and sensor bombs. The player will be provided with harness jackets, which will detect the laser. The captain will be provided with special gun and other players will be provided with pistol laser gun. The captains will be given the choice to hide the sensor bomb in the maze according to their will. The match begins with the two teams entering the maze from the opposite ends. After entering the maze, the team which triggers c4 bomb gets the advantage and has to survive the remaining time limit. The team which fails to trigger the bomb has to kill all the players of the first team in the remaining time limit. The sensor bombs which were hidden by the captains will sense the motion of players who passes through it and kill the player. Each player has 3 lives and they will respawn after 10 seconds . If the team which has triggered the bomb successfully defends itself before the c4 bomb explodes ,will win the match. If the other team manages to kill the opponent team before the explosion then that team wins the match. The team which wins will be qualified for next round and the other team will be knocked out
